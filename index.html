Choose which method of learning suits you:

[ Video Tutorials ]

Websites such as Coursera, Udemy are very popular these days. Once enrolled to a particular course, you can code along with your instructors in your own laptops and PCs. The best part about these is that you can learn at your own pace. Most of the courses also have a discussion forum to help you incase you get stuck.

[ Interactive Coding Websites ]

Websites such as Progate, Codeacademy are relatively new but can make learning very easy. They are designed so that you can code on the website itself without the hassle of setting up a development environment on your laptop.

[ Books ]

Although there are so many resources present on the internet. Some beginners still prefer the traditional way to start with books as they provide a very good base of theoretical knowledge.
